# Roadmap

This roadmap describes intent, not a delivery promise.

## v0.1.x — harden the read-only foundation

- Resolve independent security-review findings.
- Improve evidence traceability, accessibility, and deterministic fixtures.
- Expand fail-closed path, symlink, and sensitive-file tests.
- Clarify optional model-provider privacy controls.
- Add release signing and reproducible-build guidance if maintainers choose to distribute binaries.

## Future research — not started

Phase 2D.1 may be considered only through a separate design, threat review, explicit maintainer approval, and new public scope. Candidate Patch generation, generated tests, Sandbox mutation, validation-command execution, and Git workflows are not available in v0.1.0 and are not enabled by this roadmap.

## Explicit non-goals for v0.1.x

- Legacy mutation or autonomous production integration.
- Unrestricted shell or coding-Agent behavior.
- Dependency installation, deployment, or production/credential access.
- Claims of guaranteed AI correctness or verified production readiness.
